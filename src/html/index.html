With TAK, your team can get oriented quickly, make better informed decisions and quickly respond to threats. Add TAK to your operations, and make sure when your boots are on the ground, you have the tactical advantage.

The TAK capability suite is a United States Government (USG) owned product. Use of these USG owned products outside of test and evaluation require the proper authority to operate (ATO) paperwork granted by your organization’s headquarters (HQ). Before installing and/or using any of the products, ensure you are within the guidelines of your organization.

TAK-MIL is a restricted use product for USG military use only. NO distribution is authorized outside this forum except through properly vetted Program Management Offices.

ATAK-CIV is subject to the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) administered by the Department of Commerce, Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS), which advises this item is classified as EAR99. Other TAK-CIV Product Lines are currently being evaluated for commodity determination.

The TAK capability suite is a United States Government owned product. TAK-MIL is a restricted use product only available through Foreign Military Sales distribution. TAK-CIV is a restricted use product available through distribution of properly licensed commercial entities and Department of Commerce approval.
